## SWIGGY SALES PERFORMANCE DASHBOARD

##​PROJECT OVERVIEW
​THIS PROJECT INVOLVES THE END-TO-END PROCESSING AND VISUALIZATION OF A COMPREHENSIVE SWIGGY DATASET. USING MICROSOFT EXCEL, I TRANSFORMED RAW, UNSTRUCTURED DATA INTO A DYNAMIC, INTERACTIVE DASHBOARD TO ANALYZE SALES TRENDS, CUSTOMER BEHAVIOR, AND REGIONAL PERFORMANCE ACROSS INDIA.

## ​KEY INSIGHTS & FEATURES
​
THE DASHBOARD PROVIDES A HIGH-LEVEL VIEW OF KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPIS) AND GRANULAR INSIGHTS:

​EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: TRACKING TOTAL SALES (₹53.01M), TOTAL ORDERS (197.4K), AND AN AVERAGE ORDER VALUE OF ₹268.51.

​GEOGRAPHIC ANALYSIS: A CHOROPLETH MAP OF INDIA SHOWING SALES DISTRIBUTION BY STATE, HIGHLIGHTING KARNATAKA AND MAHARASHTRA AS HIGH-PERFORMING REGIONS.

​CUSTOMER PREFERENCES: A BREAKDOWN OF FOOD TYPES SHOWS THAT 65% OF ORDERS ARE VEG, INDICATING A STRONG MARKET PREFERENCE.

# TEMPORAL TRENDS:

​MONTHLY: SALES PEAKED IN JANUARY AND MAY.

​DAILY: SATURDAY IS THE HIGHEST REVENUE-GENERATING DAY (₹7.8M), FOLLOWED CLOSELY BY SUNDAY AND THURSDAY.


​TOP PERFORMERS: IDENTIFICATION OF THE TOP 5 CITIES (LED BY BENGALURU) AND THE MOST POPULAR RESTAURANT CHAINS (LED BY KFC).

​PRODUCT INSIGHTS: A "TOP 10 DISHES" CHART IDENTIFIES HIGH-VOLUME ITEMS LIKE VEG FRIED RICE AND CHOCO LAVA CAKE.

## ​DATA CLEANING & TRANSFORMATION
​
BEFORE VISUALIZATION, THE RAW DATASET UNDERWENT A RIGOROUS CLEANING PROCESS IN EXCEL TO ENSURE DATA INTEGRITY:

​REMOVING DUPLICATES: ENSURED UNIQUE ORDER IDS FOR ACCURATE COUNTING.

​STANDARDIZATION: CLEANED CITY AND RESTAURANT NAMES TO FIX SPELLING INCONSISTENCIES.

​DATA TYPE CONVERSION: CONVERTED DATE AND CURRENCY STRINGS INTO NUMERICAL FORMATS FOR CALCULATION.

​HANDLING NULLS: MANAGED MISSING VALUES IN RATINGS AND REVIEWS TO PREVENT SKEWED AVERAGES.

## ​TOOLS USED

​MICROSOFT EXCEL: DATA CLEANING, PIVOT TABLES, AND DASHBOARD DESIGN.

​POWER QUERY: FOR AUTOMATED DATA SHAPING AND TRANSFORMATION.
